Hey there :)

This is my third album. Lately, I’ve been walking a lot and somewhere along the way, I started paying attention to how I felt on those walks and I started writing those feelings down.

These songs are just that. A collection of passing thoughts, emotional leftovers, things that didn’t really have anywhere else to go.

They aren’t all pleasant. Some of them are uncomfortable. Some of them contradict each other. But they’re all real to me.

And if you’re reading this right now, I hope something here resonates with you. Even just a little.
